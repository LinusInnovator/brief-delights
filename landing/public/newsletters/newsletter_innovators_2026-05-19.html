What exactly does word2vec learn? (6 minute read)

This paper provides a quantitative theory for how the word2vec algorithm learns word embeddings, showing it reduces to unweighted least-squares matrix factorization with a closed-form solution.

Why this matters: This insight could inform the design of more efficient and interpretable language models, by leveraging the linear structure of word embeddings.
