Researchers propose StruQ and SecAlign to defend against prompt injection attacks, a major threat to LLM-integrated applications, by separating prompts from data and training LLMs to ignore injected instructions.

Why this matters: This enables safer deployment of LLMs in high-stakes applications like Google Docs and ChatGPT, mitigating a critical vulnerability.

PLAID is a multimodal generative model that learns to generate new proteins by sampling from the latent space of protein folding models, enabling control over function and organism.
