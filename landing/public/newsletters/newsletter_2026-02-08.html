PMCanalSeg: A dataset for automatic segmentation of the pterygopalatine and mandibular canals from 3D CBCT images

Researchers have created a large, publicly available dataset called PMCanalSeg that contains 191 patient cases with detailed annotations of the pterygopalatine and mandibular canals from 3D CBCT scans. This dataset addresses the challenge of high data collection costs and privacy concerns that have limited the development of deep learning models for segmenting these critical anatomical structures, which are crucial for safe orthognathic surgery.
