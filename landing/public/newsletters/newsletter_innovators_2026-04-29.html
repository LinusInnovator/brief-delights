What exactly does word2vec learn? (6 minute read)

This paper provides a quantitative theory explaining how the word2vec algorithm learns dense vector representations of words, showing it reduces to unweighted least-squares matrix factorization.

Why this matters: This fundamental insight into word2vec's learning dynamics can inform the design of more sophisticated language models and representation learning techniques.

Defending against Prompt Injection with Structured Queries (StruQ) and Preference Optimization (SecAlign) (4 minute read)

Researchers propose StruQ and SecAlign to defend against prompt injection attacks, a top threat to LLM-integrated apps, by separating prompts from data and training LLMs to ignore injected instructions.

Why this matters: These defenses enable safer deployment of LLM-powered apps, removing a critical vulnerability that could lead to malicious manipulation of LLM outputs.
