What exactly does word2vec learn? (6 minute read)

Researchers provide a quantitative theory for how word2vec learns, showing it reduces to unweighted least-squares matrix factorization with the final representations given by PCA.

Why this matters: This fundamental insight into word2vec can guide the design of more sophisticated language models and representation learning techniques.
