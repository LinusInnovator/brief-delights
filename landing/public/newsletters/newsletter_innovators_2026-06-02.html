Researchers propose StruQ and SecAlign to defend against prompt injection attacks, a top threat to LLM-integrated apps, by separating prompts from data and training LLMs to ignore injected instructions.

Why this matters: Enables LLM-powered apps to safely leverage external data without risk of malicious prompt injection, unlocking new use cases.
