RL without TD learning (9 minute read)

A new RL algorithm based on divide-and-conquer, not temporal difference learning, scales well to long-horizon tasks without TD's scalability challenges.

Why this matters: This opens new possibilities for applying RL to complex, long-horizon problems where data collection is expensive.

Adaptive Parallel Reasoning: The Next Paradigm in Efficient Inference Scaling (15 minute read)

Adaptive parallel reasoning models can dynamically decide how to decompose and parallelize reasoning tasks, improving efficiency and scaling over sequential approaches.

Why this matters: This breakthrough enables researchers to build reasoning models that can handle far more complex problems than current approaches.

The Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Blog

The Case for AI Behavioral Science (4 minute read)

The article proposes a new field of AI behavioral science to study how models develop beliefs, recover from failure, and interact in the real world.

Why this matters: This research could uncover unexpected model behaviors that challenge assumptions and inform risk mitigation strategies for advanced AI systems.

Claude Helped a Hacker Find a Way to Issue Tickets to Almost Every US Music Festival (3 minute read)

A security researcher used the AI model Claude to find a SQL injection vulnerability in Front Gate's website, exposing millions of customer records and allowing him to issue free tickets to music festivals.

Why this matters: This highlights the need for companies to thoroughly audit their systems, including using AI-powered security tools, to prevent widespread data breaches and fraud.

Defending against Prompt Injection with Structured Queries (StruQ) and Preference Optimization (SecAlign) (4 minute read)

Researchers propose StruQ and SecAlign, fine-tuning defenses that mitigate prompt injection attacks on LLMs, reducing success rates to near 0% without added cost.

Why this matters: Enables secure deployment of LLMs in high-stakes applications by addressing a critical vulnerability with practical, cost-effective solutions.
