Researchers propose SPEX and ProxySPEX, algorithms that can identify critical interactions in complex machine learning models like LLMs at scale, enabling more comprehensive model interpretability.

Repurposing Protein Folding Models for Generation with Latent Diffusion (6 minute read)

PLAID is a multimodal generative model that learns to generate new proteins by sampling the latent space of protein folding models, enabling control over function and organism.

Why this matters: This opens new possibilities for AI-driven protein engineering and drug design by allowing researchers to specify desired protein properties and generate candidates.
